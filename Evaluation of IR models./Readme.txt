The goal of the project is to implement various IR models and evaluate their performance based on Mean Average Precision(MAP) values.

A provided dataset contains twitter data in three diﬀerent languages i.e English, German and Russian along with the 15 sample queries and corresponding sample manual relevance were provided.

The Twitter data provided were at ﬁrst indexed in Solr and then evaluated by using Trec eval program.

The scores obtained gave us an idea of how relevant were the results given by our Solr instance.
